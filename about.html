The barber shop was originally launched in 1st may 2019 as a beauty shop before it was transformed to a barber shop in 3rd March 2020. We service both men and female customers and have diverse qualified barbers and other specialists offering massages and waxing services. Our story began in 2010 when our founder, Mr. Arjun, realized that there was a need for a men’s grooming destination that was dedicated to providing personalized service, attention to detail, and exceptional results. He saw an opportunity to create a barbershop that combined the traditional art of grooming with modern techniques and products, and Man Up Barbershop was born

From the very beginning, we have been committed to providing the highest level of service and expertise in haircuts, beard trims, shaves, and skincare. Our experienced team of barbers is skilled in the latest grooming techniques and uses only the highest quality products and equipment to deliver exceptional results.
